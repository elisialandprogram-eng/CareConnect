GOLDENLIFE – ANALYTICS, REPORTING & BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE CONSOLIDATION SPRINT

MISSION

Transform GoldenLife reporting into a production-grade Business Intelligence platform.

This sprint will:

1. Fix every issue identified in the Analytics & Reports Forensic Audit.
2. Create a Single Source of Truth (SSOT) for all KPIs.
3. Consolidate Admin, Provider and Patient reporting.
4. Remove duplicate calculations.
5. Remove dead analytics code.
6. Remove broken analytics APIs.
7. Remove unused reporting components.
8. Create a unified reporting architecture.
9. Improve UX/UI for all dashboards and reports.
10. Ensure all financial reporting is trustworthy.

This is the FINAL analytics architecture sprint.
