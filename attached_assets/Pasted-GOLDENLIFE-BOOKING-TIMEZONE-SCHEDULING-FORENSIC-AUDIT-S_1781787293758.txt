GOLDENLIFE — BOOKING TIMEZONE & SCHEDULING FORENSIC AUDIT SPRINT

READ FIRST

This is a P0 scheduling integrity audit.

A real-world issue has been observed:

* Provider registered in Hungary
* Patient registered in Hungary
* Booking wizard showed next available slot in ~10 minutes
* After booking, appointment appeared ~5 hours away

This indicates a possible timezone, UTC conversion, slot-generation, relative-time, or display inconsistency.

Scheduling is a core healthcare workflow.

Any mismatch between:

* Availability
* Booking
* Confirmation
* Dashboard
* Notifications
* Calendars

is considered a critical defect.

DO NOT APPLY PATCHES IMMEDIATELY.

FIRST COMPLETE A FULL FORENSIC AUDIT.

No migrations.
No backfills.
No schema changes unless absolutely required.
No temporary fixes.
No hidden timezone offsets.
No hardcoded country assumptions.
