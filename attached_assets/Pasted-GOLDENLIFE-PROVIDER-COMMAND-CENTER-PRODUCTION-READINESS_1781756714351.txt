GOLDENLIFE – PROVIDER COMMAND CENTER PRODUCTION READINESS AUDIT

Objective:

Perform a complete end-to-end audit of the newly redesigned Provider Command Center and all connected provider-management workflows.

This is NOT a feature sprint.

This is a forensic QA, UX, permissions, workflow, currency, and operational audit.

Goal:

Deliver a production-ready Provider Command Center with zero broken flows, zero dead actions, zero missing states, zero currency inconsistencies, and zero permission leaks.
