GOLDENLIFE REVENUE & PRICE DRIFT FORENSIC AUDIT

CRITICAL ISSUE

A provider configured a service price of:

5000 HUF

Yesterday.

Today a patient sees:

4981 HUF

for the same service.

This must NEVER happen.

Provider-defined service prices must be stable and immutable in their native currency.

If a provider sets:

5000 HUF

then all users should see:

5000 HUF

until the provider or admin intentionally changes that service.

The objective of this audit is to identify exactly where the price drift is occurring and permanently eliminate it.

====================================================
EXPECTED ARCHITECTURE
=====================

Provider Service Price

5000 HUF

must remain

5000 HUF

through:

* Service Listings
* Provider Profiles
* Search Results
* Booking Wizard
* Quote Engine
* Appointment Creation

Exchange rates must NOT alter provider-defined prices.

Exchange rates should only be used when:

* Creating USD accounting snapshots
* Stripe settlement
* Internal revenue reporting
* Platform accounting

NOT for displaying the provider's service price.
