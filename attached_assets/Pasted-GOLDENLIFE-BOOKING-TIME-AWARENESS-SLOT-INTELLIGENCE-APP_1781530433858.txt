SLOT VISIBILITY, COLORIZATION & BOOKING INTELLIGENCE
====================================================

The slot picker currently provides insufficient visual context.

Users should instantly understand:

* Which slots are available
* Which slots are starting soon
* Which slots require urgent attendance
* Which slot is selected
* How much time remains before the appointment starts

without performing any mental calculations.

---

## AVAILABLE FUTURE SLOTS

Green

Examples:

19:00
19:30
20:00

Meaning:

Comfortably available.

---

## STARTING SOON SLOTS

Yellow

Examples:

Current Time:
18:53

Slot:
19:15

Display:

Starts in 22 minutes

Meaning:

Appointment approaching.

Recommended threshold:

0–30 minutes.

---

## URGENT / LAST-MINUTE SLOTS

Orange

Examples:

Current Time:
18:53

Slot:
18:58

Display:

Starts in 5 minutes

Meaning:

Immediate attendance required.

Recommended threshold:

0–10 minutes.

---

## UNAVAILABLE SLOTS

Red

Examples:

Outside provider coverage

Unavailable

Booking restriction triggered

Capacity reached

Blocked availability

Meaning:

Cannot be booked.

---

## SELECTED SLOT

Blue Highlight

When a user selects a slot:

Selected state must override all other slot colors.

Example:

Selected

19:00

Starts in 7 minutes

The selected slot should be visually obvious.

---

## REAL-TIME SLOT COUNTDOWN

While booking remains open:

Countdowns must update automatically.

Examples:

Starts In:

7 Minutes

6 Minutes

5 Minutes

Use lightweight updates.

Do not introduce heavy polling.
