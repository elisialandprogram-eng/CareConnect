GOLDENLIFE – ANALYTICS & REPORTS FORENSIC AUDIT

MISSION

Perform a complete forensic audit of every analytics, reporting, KPI, dashboard, export, chart, graph, trend, summary card, and metric in the platform.

This is NOT a redesign sprint.

This is NOT a feature sprint.

This is a forensic investigation to determine:

* What is real
* What is partially correct
* What is duplicated
* What is stale
* What is fake/mock
* What is broken
* What is inconsistent
* What uses the wrong data source

The objective is to create a single source of truth for reporting across the entire platform.
