GoldenLife is still pre-launch.

The database has already been reset.

Operational data has already been removed.

There is no business requirement to preserve historical provider taxonomy values, migration compatibility layers, legacy category mappings, legacy slugs, legacy aliases, or historical catalogue naming conventions.

The objective of this sprint is to permanently normalize the platform to the final GoldenLife taxonomy and remove all remaining legacy catalogue debt, legacy compatibility code, obsolete mappings, stale values, orphan logic, and related defects.

This is a cleanup, hardening, remediation, validation, and consolidation sprint.

   slug: rehabilitation

5. Dental Care Professionals
   slug: dental
